Step 3: Plug into the LHS and set equal to RHS

\begin{align*} \frac{d^{2}y_{p}}{dt^{2}}+2\frac{dy_{p}}{dt}+10y \amp = \left[-4A\cos2t-4B\sin2t\right]\\ \amp +2\left[-2A\sin2t+2B\cos2t\right]+10\left[A\cos2t+B\sin2t\right] \end{align*}

which gives us

\begin{equation*} \left[-4A+4B+10A\right]\cos2t+\left[-4B-4A+10B\right]\sin2t=4\cos2t \end{equation*}

so that

\begin{align*} 6A+4B \amp = \amp 4\\ -4A+6B \amp = \amp 0 \end{align*}

gives us \(A=\frac{6}{13},B=\frac{4}{13}\text{.}\)
