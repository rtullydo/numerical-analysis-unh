Initially, a tank contains 100 L of water with 10 kg of sugar in solution. Water containing sugar flows into the tank at the rate of \(2\) L/min, and the well-stirred mixture in the tank flows out at the rate of \(5\) L/min. The concentration \(c(t)\) of sugar in the incoming water varies as \(c(t)=2+\cos(3t)\) kg/L. Let \(Q(t)\) be the amount of sugar (in kilograms) in the tank at time \(t\) (in minutes). Write the Initial Value Problem that \(Q(t)\) satisfies.
