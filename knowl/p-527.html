What is the largest open interval in which the solution to the IVPs in part (a) and part (b) are guaranteed to exist by the existence and uniqueness theorems?

  1. The IVP given by:
    \begin{equation*} \begin{cases} \left(t^{2}+t-2\right)y^{\prime}+e^{t}y=\frac{\left(t-4\right)}{\left(t-6\right)}\\ y(-3)=-1. \end{cases} \end{equation*}
  2. The IVP given by:
    \begin{equation*} \begin{cases} \left(t^{2}+t-2\right)y^{\prime}+e^{t}y=\frac{\left(t-4\right)}{\left(t-6\right)}\\ y(5)=47. \end{cases} \end{equation*}
