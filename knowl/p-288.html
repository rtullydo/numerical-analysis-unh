Solving exact equations: If \(Mdx+Ndy=0\) is exact then

\begin{align*} \amp \psi_{x}=M(x,y) \amp \implies \amp \psi=\int M(x,y)dx+h(y)\\ \amp \amp \amp \Downarrow\\ \amp \psi_{y}=N(x,y) \amp \amp \psi_{y}=\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(\int M(x,y)dx\right)+h^{\prime}(y) \end{align*}

and then solve for \(h(y)\text{.}\)
