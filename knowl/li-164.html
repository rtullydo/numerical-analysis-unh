Item 4.
(private) Sam wishes to sign a document \(D\) where \(1 \leq D \lt p\text{.}\) She picks a random element \(k\) with \(\gcd(k, p-1) = 1\text{.}\) She computes
\begin{equation*} S_1 = g^k \bmod p, \hspace{.5cm} S_2 = (D - aS_1)k\inv \bmod p-1. \end{equation*}
Her signature is the pair \(D^{sig} = (S_1, S_2)\text{.}\)
