But notice that

\begin{equation*} y=-\frac{1}{t+C} \end{equation*}

does NOT solve the IVP with \(y(0)=0\text{.}\) Thus we have to include the equilibrium solution \(y(t)=0\) to get the complete general solution. In this case we say the general solution is:

\begin{equation*} y_g(t) = \begin{cases} 0\\ -\frac{1}{t + C} \end{cases} \end{equation*}
