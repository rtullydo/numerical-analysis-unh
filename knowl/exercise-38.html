Exercise 1.
Determine whether each of the following equations are exact. If it is exact, find the solution. Implicit solutions are fine.
  1. \({\displaystyle \left(2x+3\right)+\left(2y-2\right)y^{\prime}=0}\)
  2. \({\displaystyle \left(2x+4y\right)+\left(2x-2y\right)y^{\prime}=0}\)
  3. \(\left(3x^{2}-2xy+2\right)dx+\left(6y^{2}-x^{2}+3\right)dy=0\)
  4. \({\displaystyle \left(2xy^{2}+2y\right)+\left(2x^{2}y+2x\right)y^{\prime}=0}\)
  5. \({\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}=-\frac{ax+by}{bx+cy}}\)
  6. \({\displaystyle \left(e^{x}\sin y+3y\right)dx-\left(3x-e^{x}\sin y\right)dy=0}\)
  7. \({\displaystyle \left(\frac{y}{x}+6x\right)dx+\left(\ln x-2\right)dy=0},\)\(x>0\)
