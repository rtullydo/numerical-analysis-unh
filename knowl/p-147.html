Now suppose that \([a,b]\) is partitioned into \(n\) segments of equal length \(\Delta x = \frac{b - a}{n}\text{,}\) where \(n\) is even (which will allow overlapping sequences of three points). Then for three sequential points \(x_i, x_{i+1}, x_{i+2}\text{,}\) the previous computation gives

\begin{equation*} \int_{x_i}^{x_{i+2}} f(x) \, dx \approx \frac{\Delta x}{3}\left(f(x_i) + 4f(x_{i+1}) + f(x_{i+2})\right). \end{equation*}

Since we chose \(n\) to be even, we get

\begin{align*} \int_a^b f(x)\, dx \amp\approx \frac{\Delta x}{3}\left(f(x_0) + 4f(x_{1}) + f(x_{2})\right) + \ldots + \frac{\Delta x}{3}\left(f(x_{n-2}) + 4f(x_{n-1}) + f(x_{n})\right)\\ \amp\approx \frac{\Delta x}{3} \left(f(x_0) + 4 \sum_{i \text{ odd}} f(x_i)) + 2 \sum_{i \text{ even}} f(x_i) + f(x_n)\right) \end{align*}
