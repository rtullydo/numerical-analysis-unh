The table below summarizes the solutions for homogeneous linear second order differential equations with constant coefficients.

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{|c|c|c|} \hline \text{roots}: \amp \text{general solution} \amp \text{example}\\ \hline r_{1},r_{2}= \text{real, distinct} \amp y(t)=c_{1}e^{r_{1}t}+c_{2}e^{r_{2}t} \amp \left(r+1\right)\left(r-1\right)=0 \\ \hline r=a\pm ib,\text{imaginary} \amp y(t)=c_{1}e^{a t}\cos b t+c_{2}e^{a t}\sin b t \amp r^{2}+1=0\\ \hline r=r_{1},\text{real, repeated} \amp y(t)=c_{1}e^{r_{1}t}+c_{2}te^{r_{1}t}. \amp \left(r-2\right)^{2}=0\\ \hline \end{array} \end{equation*}
