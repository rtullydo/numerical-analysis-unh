Item 4.
(private) Sam wishes to sign a document \(D\) where \(1 \leq D \lt q\text{.}\) She picks a random element \(1 \lt k \lt q\text{.}\) She computes
\begin{equation*} S_1 = g^k \bmod q, \hspace{.5cm} S_2 = (D - aS_1)k\inv \bmod q. \end{equation*}
Her signature is the pair \(D^{sig} = (S_1, S_2)\text{.}\)
