Suppose \(\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{-2x-y^{2}}{2xy}.\) We can rewrite this as \(\left(2x+y^{2}\right)dx+2xydy=0\) then \(M=2x+y^{2}\) and \(N=2xy\text{.}\) Computing the partial derivatives,
\begin{align*} M_{y} \amp =2y\\ N_{x} \amp =2y \end{align*}
are \(M_{y}=N_{x}\text{.}\) Thus this equation is exact.
