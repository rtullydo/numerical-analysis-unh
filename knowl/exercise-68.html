Exercise 7.

For the following ODEs. Use the method of undertermined coefficients (MOUC) to make the correct guess for the \(y_{p}\text{.}\) You DO NOT have to solve for the coefficients, \(A,B,C\dots\text{.}\) Simply make the correct guess for the \(y_{p}\text{.}\)

  1. \(y^{\prime\prime}-2y^{\prime}+y=te^{t},\)
  2. \(y^{\prime\prime}+y^{\prime}-2y=t^{2}e^{t},\)
  3. \(y^{\prime\prime}+y^{\prime}=t^{2}+\cos t,\)
  4. \(y^{\prime\prime}+y^{\prime}-6y=e^{5t}+\sin(3t),\)
  5. \(y^{\prime\prime}+y^{\prime}-2y=te^{t}+t^{2},\)
