Consider a pond that initially constains \(10\) million gal of pure water. Water containing a polluted chemical flows into the pond at the rate of \(6\) million gal/year, and the mixture in the pond flows out at the rate of \(5\) million gal/year. The concentration \(\gamma(t)\) of chemical in the incoming water varies as \(\gamma(t)=2+\sin2t\) grams/gal. Let \(Q(t)\) be the amount of chemical at time \(t\) measured by millions of grams. What is the IVP that \(Q(t)\) satisfies?
