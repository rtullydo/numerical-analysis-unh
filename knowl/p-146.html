So in effect, we're saying that

\begin{align*} \int_a^b f(x)\, dx \amp \approx \int_a^b b_0 + b_1 (x - a) + b_2 (x -a) (x - (\frac{a+b}{2}))\\ \amp = \ldots\\ \amp= \frac{\Delta x}{3}\left( f(a) + 4f(\frac{a+b}{2}) + f(b)\right) \end{align*}

where we have shoved integration and substitution under the rug (or into the ellipsis, as it were).
