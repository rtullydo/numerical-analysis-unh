Item 2.
As an encryption function, Alice uses multiplication by \(k \bmod p\text{.}\) She knows that multiplication is one-to-one, since \(\F_p\ad\) is a field, so each message \(m \in \F_p\ad\) will be sent to a unique \(c \in \F_p\ad\) by the function
\begin{equation*} e_k(m) = k\cdot m \bmod p. \end{equation*}
